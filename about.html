About IslandWatch

IslandWatch helps you keep track of the things that get you around Bermuda. We start with bikes, then cars, company fleets, and the gear businesses rely on. You get a small tracking device fitted to your vehicle and an app that shows you where it is, warns you if something is wrong, and helps you act fast. One platform, one local team.

lightbulb

Why we built it

Bike theft is something almost every rider in Bermuda has dealt with, or worried about. A bike disappears, and there is no easy way to find it. IslandWatch changes that. You are the first to know if your bike is moved or tampered with, you can see its location on a live map, and you can share that location with the Bermuda Police Service if it is ever stolen.

handyman

How we work

We are a small Bermuda team, not an overseas call centre. The same team fits the device, sets up your app, and answers your questions, from start to finish. Devices are installed by hand and built to be hard to remove without setting off an alert. When you need help, you are talking to people who live here.

More than theft protection

The device that protects a parked bike does more once you are on the road:

speed

New riders (and their parents) get a weekly Safe Driving Score: one simple number, in plain words, based on things like speeding, harsh acceleration, harsh braking, and collision detection.

local_shipping

Businesses see all of their vehicles on one screen, get alerts when a vehicle leaves a set area, and can look back over past trips.

inventory_2

Trailers, generators, and other valuable equipment get the same protection, in the same app.

Where we are headed

We are starting with a small group of riders, a pilot, so we can get everything right on real Bermuda roads before we grow. After that comes IslandWatch Fleet for businesses, then tracking for cars and other vehicles. It is one platform that grows with the island.
